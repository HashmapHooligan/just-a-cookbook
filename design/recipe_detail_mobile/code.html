Rustic Shakshuka

Prep Time 15min
Total Time 35min
Yield 4srv

Ingredients

  • 🥚 4 large eggs
  • 🍅 1 can (28oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 🧅 1 medium onion, diced
  • 🫑 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 🧄 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 🌶️ 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 🌿 Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

1

Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add a splash of olive oil, then sauté the diced onion and red bell pepper until soft, about 5 minutes.

2

Stir in the minced garlic and smoked paprika. Cook for 1 more minute until fragrant, being careful not to let the garlic burn.

3

Pour in the crushed tomatoes. Season with salt and pepper. Let the mixture simmer for 10-15 minutes until it thickens slightly.

4

Make 4 small wells in the sauce. Crack an egg into each well. Cover the skillet and cook for 5-8 minutes, or until the egg whites are set but yolks are still runny. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve immediately.
